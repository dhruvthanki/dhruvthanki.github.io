Dhruv Thanki

I am a Master of Science in Robotics student at Department of Mechanical Engineering at University of Delaware, where I have started working on control and dynamics of legged robots under the guidance of Dr. Ioannis Poulakakis. In the past, I have worked at Space Application Center - ISRO as a Robotics Project Intern on formulating a control system and a closed form solution to the inverse kinematics of a Walk-And-Roll Rover leg.

I grew up in Porbandar City, India, a small coastal town in the state of Gujarat. Since then, I've lived in Jinja (Uganda), Ahmedabad (Gujarat), and now living in Newark (Delaware). The things that I am very passionate about are Street Photography and Rock music.
